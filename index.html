New This Morning: Power Automate as the Durable Workflow Layer

A key insight from this session was identifying how OpenClaw and Power Automate complement one another inside a Microsoft-first operating model.

What Was Clarified

The Teams Workflows app is effectively a Microsoft-native front door into Power Automate. That makes it a strong destination for persistent, recurring workflows identified through natural conversation with the agent.

Why It Matters

Instead of leaving repeated operational patterns trapped in chat context, they can be confirmed, formalized, and promoted into durable workflow infrastructure inside the Microsoft ecosystem.

Why Teams as a Command Surface Matters

Teams is where decisions and coordination already happen. Using it as a command surface brings operational execution directly into the conversation loop—reducing handoff latency, increasing visibility, and enabling faster outcomes from intent to delivered artifact.

For modern organizations, this means leadership, operations, and engineering can collaborate in one familiar interface while AI agents handle the heavy lifting across tools and systems.
